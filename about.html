About

Hi! My name is Eunice Yiu. I am a fifth-year PhD candidate in Psychology at UC Berkeley. My research interests intersect between child cognitive development and artificial intelligence. I conduct research at the Cognitive Development and Learning Lab under the supervision of Dr. Alison Gopnik.

I graduated from Cornell University in 2020, triple majoring in Psychology, Biological Sciences and Economics. I am an active and curious learner of many things.
