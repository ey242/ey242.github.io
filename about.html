About

Hi! My name is Eunice Yiu. I am a fifth-year PhD candidate in Psychology at UC Berkeley advised by Dr. Alison Gopnik.

My research interests intersect between child cognitive development and artificial intelligence, where I study how children achieve flexible learning and generalization, focusing on analogical reasoning and intrinsic learning signals that drive exploration and causal interventions, comparing their abilities to AI models.
