Research Interests

1. Structured World-Model Evaluation:

Designing controlled testbeds to evaluate causal, spatial, and compositional generalization in multimodal and embodied AI systems.

2. Learning under Constraints (Active vs. Passive Data):

Analyzing how humans and AI agents acquire information, revise hypotheses, and refine internal models through intervention-based and observational learning settings.

3. Human–AI Co-Adaptation:

Assessing how model behavior affects human reasoning and decision-making, and how diverse human goals and values in turn influence system behavior in interactive settings.
