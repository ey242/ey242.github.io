Research Interests

I am interested in how children and AI systems achieve efficient, flexible learning from limited data, and what this tells us about the principles of human cognition and the design of better AI.

My research interests can be categorized into the following three strands:

1. Abstraction & Analogy:

How do learners detect structure, form analogies, and generalize beyond surface features?

2. Active Exploration:

How do intrinsic motivations like information gain and empowerment drive learners to intervene and build causal models?

3. Curriculum Learning:

How does the sequencing of experiences shape learning trajectories and supports generalization?
